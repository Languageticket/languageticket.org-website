How It Works

Simple steps to begin your personalized English learning experience

1

Online Application

Complete our quick application form to share your goals and preferences. This takes about 90 seconds.

2

Free Assessment

We'll conduct a free evaluation within 72 hours to understand your current level and learning style.

3

Teacher Matching

We match you with a qualified teacher based on your needs. Onboarding is completed within one week.

4

Begin Learning

Start your personalized English lessons and track your progress with regular feedback.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our programs and processes

We conduct assessments within 72 hours and complete onboarding within one week of application.

We match students with teachers based on compatibility. Both students and teachers can request a change if the match isn't working well.

You can access lessons from any smartphone, tablet, or laptop with a stable internet connection.

You can reschedule sessions with at least 72 hours advance notice at no additional cost.

Our teachers have 3-5 years of experience on average and hold relevant teaching certifications.

We offer group classes for general English programs, but skill-based courses are one-on-one. Organizations can request custom group programs.

We accept students as young as 4 years old, with no upper age limit.

Cancellations made 72+ hours in advance are free. Hourly-based packages have a 7-day refund policy. Course-based packages offer partial refunds.

We do not currently offer financial aid or scholarships.

Most of our teachers hold relevant qualifications, but not all have advanced degrees.

Teacher Qualifications

Our educators meet rigorous standards to ensure quality instruction

Education & Certification

  • Bachelor's degree or equivalent in relevant field
  • TEFL/TESOL certification (minimum 120 hours)
  • Specialized training in our methodology
  • Ongoing professional development

Teaching Experience

  • 3-5 years of teaching experience on average
  • Experience with diverse student populations
  • Proven track record of student success
  • Specializations in test prep or business English

Personal Qualities

  • Excellent communication skills
  • Patience and adaptability
  • Cultural sensitivity and awareness
  • Passion for teaching and student success
